# 你现在可以怎么用这个框架

你可以统一套用这个模板来构思：

## 正面模板

* **X in daily life**
* **X in academia**
* **X in work**
* **X in personal development**
* **X in society**

## 负面模板

* **negative effects in daily life**
* **negative effects in academia**
* **negative effects in work**
* **negative effects in mental/physical wellbeing**
* **negative effects in society**

这样你以后看到任何题目，都可以迅速套进去。

例如题目如果是：

**“Computers are becoming more important in education.”**

你就可以这样构思：

* **Technology in academia**

  * expand access to knowledge
  * support personalised learning
* **Efficiency in academia**

  * accelerate research and revision
* **Health / Interpersonal negative side**

  * reduce face-to-face interaction
  * contribute to eye strain and mental fatigue
* **Success in academia**

  * improve academic performance
  * or create overdependence and shallow learning

# 背诵顺序建议

如果你的目标是尽快把这套材料真正用起来，建议按下面的顺序背：

1. **先背 CHEMIST 七大类的正面 / 负面主方向**
2. **再背每一类常见子类**
   * academia
   * work
   * daily life
   * society
   * personal development
3. **然后练习高覆盖题材**
   * Education
   * Computer & Internet
   * Occupation
   * Mass Media
4. **最后集中背 TOPICS.md 里的 7 个弱覆盖题材**

这样背的好处是：

* 先把最通用的框架背熟
* 再补最容易卡壳的话题
* 记忆负担不会一下子过重
* 真正写作时更容易快速调用
