# TOPICS

这一份只收录 **CHEMIST 不够顺手、但 IELTS Task 2 很常见** 的题材。

背诵时不要把它当成复杂的知识体系，而要把它当成一份 **题材素材册**。

每个题材都按同一个模板来背：

* **题材核心矛盾**
* **正面常用思路**
* **负面常用思路**
* **高频词组**
* **万能句**

---

## 1. Animals

### 题材核心矛盾

人类是否应该为了经济利益、科学研究或娱乐需求而使用动物，还是应该把动物福利和生态保护放在更重要的位置。

### 正面常用思路

* protecting animals reflects human responsibility towards other living creatures
* preserving wildlife helps maintain ecological balance
* stricter protection can reduce cruelty and improve public morality

### 负面常用思路

* some controlled use of animals is defended for scientific or medical progress
* zoos and breeding programmes may help conserve endangered species
* overly rigid restrictions may ignore practical needs in farming and research

### 高频词组

* animal welfare
* biodiversity conservation
* ecological balance
* ethical treatment of animals
* animal experimentation
* endangered species
* captivity and confinement
* human responsibility

### 万能句

* A strong case for animal protection is that human beings have a moral responsibility to treat other living creatures in a humane manner.
* Critics argue that animals should not be exploited merely for profit, entertainment or scientific convenience.

## 2. Environment

### 题材核心矛盾

经济发展和生活便利往往会带来环境压力，因此核心问题是，人类应该优先追求增长，还是优先追求可持续发展。

### 正面常用思路

* environmental protection supports long-term public wellbeing
* cleaner energy and greener habits can reduce pollution
* sustainable policies help preserve resources for future generations

### 负面常用思路

* environmental measures may increase costs for businesses and households
* rapid green transition can put pressure on traditional industries
* some people prioritise short-term convenience over ecological responsibility

### 高频词组

* environmental degradation
* air and water pollution
* climate change
* sustainable development
* renewable energy
* conservation efforts
* carbon emissions
* consumer responsibility

### 万能句

* One major reason for prioritising environmental protection is that short-term economic gains should not come at the expense of long-term sustainability.
* The main concern is that unchecked industrial and consumer activity can cause irreversible damage to the natural environment.

## 3. Language & Culture

### 题材核心矛盾

全球化和国际交流带来便利，但也可能削弱本地语言、传统文化和文化认同。

### 正面常用思路

* cultural exchange broadens people’s horizons
* bilingualism can improve communication and opportunities
* learning foreign languages helps international cooperation

### 负面常用思路

* dominant global languages may weaken minority languages
* cultural homogenisation can erode local traditions
* the loss of heritage may reduce a community’s sense of identity

### 高频词组

* cultural identity
* cultural preservation
* linguistic diversity
* heritage protection
* cultural homogenisation
* language loss
* bilingual ability
* global communication

### 万能句

* Supporters often argue that exposure to different languages and cultures can broaden people’s perspectives and strengthen global understanding.
* Opponents warn that excessive global influence may gradually undermine local traditions and cultural identity.

## 4. Government

### 题材核心矛盾

政府应不应该积极干预社会问题，还是应该把更多选择权留给个人、家庭和市场。

### 正面常用思路

* government intervention can protect the public interest
* public spending can improve education, healthcare and infrastructure
* regulation is sometimes necessary to prevent harm and unfairness

### 负面常用思路

* excessive intervention may limit individual freedom
* inefficient public spending can waste taxpayers’ money
* too many regulations may create dependence or reduce flexibility

### 高频词组

* public spending
* state intervention
* government regulation
* taxpayers’ money
* social welfare
* public interest
* individual freedom
* policy implementation

### 万能句

* A common argument in favour of government action is that certain social problems are too large to be solved by individuals alone.
* The opposing view is that excessive state intervention may restrict personal choice and reduce individual responsibility.

## 5. Travel & Tourism

### 题材核心矛盾

旅游能带来经济收益和文化交流，但也可能造成环境压力、商业化和本地生活负担。

### 正面常用思路

* tourism can create jobs and stimulate local economies
* travel encourages cross-cultural understanding
* well-managed tourism can support heritage preservation

### 负面常用思路

* overtourism may damage the environment and local infrastructure
* popular destinations can become overly commercialised
* local residents may face higher living costs and crowded public spaces

### 高频词组

* tourism revenue
* cultural exchange
* overtourism
* heritage preservation
* seasonal employment
* environmental pressure
* commercialisation of local culture
* responsible travel

### 万能句

* The main benefit of tourism is that it can generate income while also promoting mutual understanding between people from different backgrounds.
* The main drawback is that uncontrolled tourism may place heavy pressure on local communities, cultural sites and the natural environment.

## 6. Nation

### 题材核心矛盾

一个国家在追求国际竞争力和全球参与的同时，是否还能保持自身认同、文化特色和长远利益。

### 正面常用思路

* national development can raise living standards and global influence
* investment in talent and innovation strengthens competitiveness
* a strong national image can attract tourism, trade and cooperation

### 负面常用思路

* brain drain can weaken long-term national development
* excessive global dependence may reduce cultural or economic autonomy
* unhealthy nationalism may create division or hostility

### 高频词组

* national identity
* global competitiveness
* brain drain
* international reputation
* national development
* cultural sovereignty
* skilled workforce
* long-term national interest

### 万能句

* A nation can only achieve sustainable development if it invests in its people, protects its long-term interests and remains competitive internationally.
* A major concern is that the pursuit of global success may weaken national identity or encourage the loss of talented citizens to other countries.

## 7. Sports

### 题材核心矛盾

体育既可以促进健康、教育和社会凝聚力，也可能因为商业化和竞争压力而偏离其原本价值。

### 正面常用思路

* sport promotes physical health and self-discipline
* team sports can strengthen cooperation and social bonds
* investment in sports can support youth development and national pride

### 负面常用思路

* excessive competition may put young people under pressure
* commercialisation can make sport less accessible and less pure
* unequal facilities may widen gaps between social groups

### 高频词组

* physical wellbeing
* sportsmanship
* youth development
* national pride
* competitive pressure
* commercialisation of sport
* access to sports facilities
* team spirit

### 万能句

* One clear advantage of sport is that it promotes not only physical wellbeing but also discipline, teamwork and perseverance.
* The main criticism is that when sport becomes overly commercialised, health and character-building may be replaced by profit and competitive pressure.
