可以。你这里的“1个万能句”，最好不是那种**看起来高级但只能用于一个题目**的句子，而是那种：

* 能适配 **利 / 弊 / 双边讨论**
* 能嵌入 **CHEMIST 任意一类**
* 能自然替换成 **academia / work / daily life / society** 等子分类

所以我建议你把“万能句”分成三层来准备：

1. **总起万能句**：用于提出某个维度的好处或坏处
2. **分类万能句**：用于细分成 academia / work / daily life 等
3. **结果万能句**：用于说明进一步影响

这样最实用。

---

# 一、最核心的万能句框架

这是你整个 CHEMIST 框架最值得背的母句型：

## 正面万能句

**One major advantage is that it can enhance [CHEMIST维度], particularly in terms of [子类], thereby contributing to [结果].**

例如：

* One major advantage is that it can enhance **efficiency**, particularly in terms of **workplace productivity**, thereby contributing to **better professional performance**.
* One major advantage is that it can enhance **convenience**, particularly in terms of **daily life**, thereby contributing to **a more manageable routine**.

这个句型几乎可以套全部正面论点。

---

## 负面万能句

**One notable drawback is that it may undermine [CHEMIST维度], particularly in the context of [子类], thereby leading to [结果].**

例如：

* One notable drawback is that it may undermine **health**, particularly in the context of **mental wellbeing**, thereby leading to **heightened stress and emotional exhaustion**.
* One notable drawback is that it may undermine **interpersonal relationships**, particularly in the context of **family interaction**, thereby leading to **emotional distance among family members**.

---

# 二、适合你分类思路的万能句

你想要的是像 **success in academia / success in work** 这样继续细分。那你最好背下面这组。

---

## 1. 用来引出“某一类中的某个子类”

**This is especially evident in [子类], where it can [具体作用].**

例如：

* This is especially evident in **academia**, where it can **facilitate access to educational resources**.
* This is especially evident in **the workplace**, where it can **improve operational efficiency**.

这个句子非常万能，几乎每段正文都能接第二句。

---

## 2. 用来表示“在A方面体现为……”

**In terms of [子类], it can be argued that it [作用].**

例如：

* In terms of **daily life**, it can be argued that it **reduces the burden of routine tasks**.
* In terms of **mental health**, it can be argued that it **alleviates psychological strain**.

这个更书面，也更适合 Task 2。

---

## 3. 用来表示“在学业 / 工作 / 社会层面”

**At the level of [academia / work / society / personal development], its influence is particularly significant.**

例如：

* At the level of **academia**, its influence is particularly significant.
* At the level of **personal development**, its influence is particularly significant.

这句适合用来开子论点。

# 三、CHEMIST 每一类都配 1 个“高适配万能句”

下面我给你每一类都配一个可以反复套用的主句。

---

## C — Convenience

### 正面

**One clear benefit is that it enhances convenience in [子类] by reducing the time and effort required for [活动].**

例如：

* One clear benefit is that it enhances convenience in **daily life** by reducing the time and effort required for **routine tasks**.
* One clear benefit is that it enhances convenience in **education** by reducing the time and effort required for **accessing learning materials**.

### 负面

**However, excessive reliance on such convenience may erode people’s ability to [能力], particularly in [子类].**

例如：

* However, excessive reliance on such convenience may erode people’s ability to **function independently**, particularly in **daily life**.
* However, excessive reliance on such convenience may erode people’s ability to **engage in independent inquiry**, particularly in **academia**.

---

## H — Health

### 正面

**Another significant advantage is that it can promote [physical / mental] wellbeing in [子类] by [机制].**

例如：

* Another significant advantage is that it can promote **mental wellbeing** in **the workplace** by **reducing occupational strain**.
* Another significant advantage is that it can promote **physical wellbeing** in **daily life** by **encouraging healthier habits**.

### 负面

**Its most serious disadvantage, however, is that it may take a toll on [physical / mental] health, especially in [子类].**

例如：

* Its most serious disadvantage, however, is that it may take a toll on **mental health**, especially in **academic settings**.
* Its most serious disadvantage, however, is that it may take a toll on **physical health**, especially in **sedentary lifestyles**.

---

## E — Efficiency

### 正面

**From the perspective of efficiency, it can help individuals or institutions to [动作] in a more streamlined and productive manner.**

例如：

* From the perspective of efficiency, it can help individuals or institutions to **complete tasks** in a more streamlined and productive manner.
* From the perspective of efficiency, it can help individuals or institutions to **manage resources** in a more streamlined and productive manner.

### 负面

**Nevertheless, an excessive emphasis on efficiency may come at the expense of [quality / wellbeing / creativity], particularly in [子类].**

例如：

* Nevertheless, an excessive emphasis on efficiency may come at the expense of **deep learning**, particularly in **academia**.
* Nevertheless, an excessive emphasis on efficiency may come at the expense of **employee wellbeing**, particularly in **high-pressure workplaces**.

---

## M — Money

### 正面

**Financially speaking, it can be beneficial because it either reduces [cost] or increases [income/opportunity] in [子类].**

例如：

* Financially speaking, it can be beneficial because it either reduces **household expenditure** or increases **economic opportunity** in **daily life**.
* Financially speaking, it can be beneficial because it either reduces **educational costs** or increases **career prospects** in **academia and work**.

### 负面

**On the other hand, it may impose a financial burden on [群体], especially when [条件].**

例如：

* On the other hand, it may impose a financial burden on **low-income families**, especially when **substantial initial investment is required**.
* On the other hand, it may impose a financial burden on **students**, especially when **essential resources are not equally affordable**.

---

## I — Interpersonal Relationships & Relaxation

### 正面

**In social terms, it can strengthen [family bonds / peer relationships / workplace rapport] by creating more opportunities for [互动].**

例如：

* In social terms, it can strengthen **family bonds** by creating more opportunities for **meaningful interaction**.
* In social terms, it can strengthen **peer relationships** by creating more opportunities for **communication and mutual support**.

### 负面

**Despite this, it may weaken genuine human interaction by encouraging [superficial contact / isolation / distraction], especially in [子类].**

例如：

* Despite this, it may weaken genuine human interaction by encouraging **superficial contact**, especially in **online communication**.
* Despite this, it may weaken genuine human interaction by encouraging **social withdrawal**, especially in **personal life**.

### relaxation 正面专用

**It can also provide a valuable source of relaxation, allowing people in [子类] to recover from [pressure].**

例如：

* It can also provide a valuable source of relaxation, allowing people in **academic settings** to recover from **intense study pressure**.
* It can also provide a valuable source of relaxation, allowing people in **the workplace** to recover from **occupational stress**.

### relaxation 负面专用

**However, when taken to excess, such leisure may become a form of escapism rather than genuine recovery.**

这句很好背，也很高级。

---

## S — Success

这是你最常用的一类，我给你多配几句。

### 正面

**Perhaps its most valuable contribution is that it can foster success in [academia / work / personal development] by improving [能力/结果].**

例如：

* Perhaps its most valuable contribution is that it can foster success in **academia** by improving **learning efficiency and confidence**.
* Perhaps its most valuable contribution is that it can foster success in **work** by improving **professional competence and productivity**.

### 负面

**Conversely, its absence or misuse may result in failure in [academia / work], as individuals become less able to [能力].**

例如：

* Conversely, its absence or misuse may result in failure in **academia**, as individuals become less able to **cope with academic demands**.
* Conversely, its absence or misuse may result in failure in **work**, as individuals become less able to **perform effectively in professional contexts**.

### 学业成功专用

**In academic contexts, this may translate into better performance, greater confidence, and broader educational opportunities.**

### 工作成功专用

**In professional contexts, this may lead to stronger job performance, enhanced career prospects, and greater social contribution.**

### 学业失败专用

**In education, the negative consequences may include underachievement, discouragement, and a reduced sense of academic self-worth.**

### 工作失败专用

**In the workplace, the adverse effects may range from occupational stress to poor performance and limited career progression.**

---

## T — Technology

### 正面

**From a technological standpoint, it can enhance [education / work / healthcare / communication] by enabling [功能].**

例如：

* From a technological standpoint, it can enhance **education** by enabling **faster access to information**.
* From a technological standpoint, it can enhance **healthcare** by enabling **more accurate diagnosis and treatment**.

### 负面

**However, growing dependence on technology may create problems in [子类], ranging from [问题1] to [问题2].**

例如：

* However, growing dependence on technology may create problems in **academia**, ranging from **reduced concentration** to **intellectual passivity**.
* However, growing dependence on technology may create problems in **society**, ranging from **privacy concerns** to **digital inequality**.

# 四、你最值得背的“结果句”

因为万能句最好不是只说“有利/有害”，还要能接结果。下面这些你可以和所有 CHEMIST 主句搭配。

## 正面结果句

* **thereby contributing to greater overall wellbeing**
* **which in turn improves people’s quality of life**
* **thus leading to more favourable academic or professional outcomes**
* **thereby promoting both individual development and social progress**
* **which may ultimately enhance long-term productivity and stability**

## 负面结果句

* **thereby giving rise to a range of negative consequences**
* **which may ultimately undermine long-term development**
* **thus creating both personal and social problems**
* **thereby diminishing people’s wellbeing and performance**
* **which may, in the long run, do more harm than good**

# 五、直接给你一套“万能句模板库”

你可以直接背这些。

## 模板 1：总起正面

**One major advantage is that it can enhance [CHEMIST维度], particularly in [子类], thereby contributing to [结果].**

## 模板 2：总起负面

**One major drawback is that it may undermine [CHEMIST维度], particularly in [子类], thereby leading to [结果].**

## 模板 3：细分类

**This is especially evident in [子类], where it can [具体作用].**

## 模板 4：学业类

**In academic contexts, this may result in [结果1], [结果2], and [结果3].**

## 模板 5：工作类

**In professional settings, this may lead to [结果1], [结果2], and [结果3].**

## 模板 6：社会类

**At the societal level, its impact can be seen in [社会结果].**

## 模板 7：让步转折

**Although this may bring certain benefits, it can also generate serious concerns in terms of [维度].**

## 模板 8：双边讨论

**While it can undoubtedly promote [正面维度], it may simultaneously undermine [负面维度].**

这句特别适合利弊题。

# 六、最推荐你优先背的 8 句

如果你不想背太多，先背这 8 句就够实战了：

1. **One major advantage is that it can enhance [维度], particularly in [子类], thereby contributing to [结果].**
2. **One notable drawback is that it may undermine [维度], particularly in [子类], thereby leading to [结果].**
3. **This is especially evident in [子类], where it can [具体作用].**
4. **In academic contexts, this may translate into [结果].**
5. **In professional settings, this may lead to [结果].**
6. **At the societal level, its impact is particularly significant.**
7. **While it can undoubtedly promote [正面], it may also give rise to [负面].**
8. **In the long run, this may affect not only [个人层面] but also [社会层面].**

# 七、给你一个最适合 CHEMIST 的“完整版万能句公式”

你以后可以这样现套：

**One major advantage/drawback is that it can enhance/undermine [CHEMIST维度], particularly in [子类], where it may [具体作用], thereby leading to [直接结果] and, in the long run, [更宏观结果].**

例如：

**One major advantage is that it can enhance efficiency, particularly in academic settings, where it may facilitate quicker access to information, thereby leading to better learning outcomes and, in the long run, greater educational achievement.**

这个就是你框架的终极母句。
